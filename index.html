FAQs

Q: Are my children safe using a web camera online?

A: Don't worry about your child's safety. The technology that we use runs in your browser, no data ever leaves your computer. That means, everything is safe to use in the home, or wherever you choose to NeuToy.

Q: What age range is NeuToy suitable for?

A: NeuToy is designed for elementary students as young as 8 years old. Big kids and little kids can play together, on their own, or with an adult. Our content is safe and does not contain violent, graphic or inappropriate themes.
